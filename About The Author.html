About The Author

I, Sarju Poudel, am the author of contents found on this website. I graduated high school in 2015 from New Capital Higher Secondary School and explored several educational opportunities. I finally settled down with US education and graudated with Bachelor of Mechanical Engineering from the University of Texas at Tyler in May 2022. The information on this website has been compiled based on my personal experience and experiences that I gathered from adivising other students who reached out to me over the course of 6 years. I had good success with providing relevant information to graduating high school students and landing them in one of their choosen US college with Full Tuition+ scholarships. Receiving positive feedbacks and success from other students, I have decided to make college application information available publicly. I congratulate you on embarking in this new journey of higher education pursuit and wish you a good luck for your future.

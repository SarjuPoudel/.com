Preparing Your Departure: Recap . . .

What To Carry on Travel Date

  • Passport With VISA
  • Initial I20 reflecting all your fees and scholarship.
  • No Objection Letter from Ministry of Education. (Located at Lainchaur, Kathmandu)
  • Original Academic Documents like transcripts, provisional certificates, marksheets, and migration certifications
  • One USEF Attested copies of academic documents.
  • Proof of your health insurance payment.
  • Bank money order slip, if any.
  • Original Nepali Citizenship. Required to renew your passport from US
  • Educational Board Attested document along with WES evaluation, just in case.

What To Plan Before Arrival

  • Plan your stay, if you are coming early.
  • Plan your airport pickup.
  • If no one is available for pickup, contact someone who can arrange UBER ride
  • Bring a phone with WIFI Capabilities or roaming services for emergency purpose.
  • Bring a round to flat multiplug if necessary for charging your phones and electronic equipment.
  • You can carry upto $2500 cash. Bring some cash for initial expenses.

What not to Bring

  • Cooking Utensils. (Can be found in amazon at reasonable price)
  • Too many cloths. Cloths brought from Nepal may get damaged after washing and drying in machines.
  • To many shoes from Nepal. They don't last here long.
  • Bring a phone (with WIFI Capabilities) for emergency purpose.
  • Bring a round to flat multiplug if necessary for charging your phones and electronic equipment.
  • You can carry upto $2500 cash. Bring some cash for initial expenses.

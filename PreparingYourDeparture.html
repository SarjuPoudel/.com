Preparing Your Departure

Yahoo! You are all set. Your hard work and sleepless nights finally paid off and you are now ready to embark on your long sought dream of attending the US College. While there is no way to fully prepare departing from your home and family for next four year, there are few things that help your transition to your new home.

If it is your first time leaving your home, you should prepare for lots of surprises, funs, and uncertainities. First research about the area of college. Revisit your living plans while attending the college. Reach out to other international students or students from your country and may be pair yourself with a compatible roommate. Pack cloths for the season and take some extra cash for emergency purpose.

It is also important to plan through which port of entry will you get into United States. Did you make airport pickup arrangement with college or someone else? If you are travelling early, where will you stay and how do you plan to spend? Carry a smartphone with you for emergency to contact your friends and family. Connect with Nepali Network group on social media.

On the day of Departure, check if you carried your passport, visa, I20, original academic documents, attested copies of academic document, proof of insurance, and No Objection Letter from Nepal Government. First time traveller may be interivewed in the transit as well as at the port of entry. Just answer their questions politely and you will be on your way.

For more information, on predeparture please visit USEF Nepal or Department of State's Travel Information

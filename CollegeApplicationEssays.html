College Application: Essays

Essays are the important part of any college application. Almost all college application has at least a question to answer. These questions ask you to give more information about you, so the admission officer can know you better. If you are using third party application services like Common App, you will find a section where you type out your essay response to one or more questions.

There is no standard on how to write an essay, therefore, you have a complete autonomy of your essay content and organization. Essays can be of any form. You can write essays about you, your community, your achievement, your future plan, your vision, or your experience. No matter what topic you select, the essay should showcase your potential to be a successful student and a contributing member to the University. For inspiration on how to write essay, you may check out essays that have been accepted to top college. These essay are published online and eassily accessible.

Writing essay is not an easy task. You have experienced so many things in life to get to where you are at now. It is quite challenging to put in words about how you envision yourself and your surroundings. But don't get discoraged. Writing a good college essay needs a persistent effort and regular practise. It may take a long time and several drafts before coming to your final script of your college essay.

    Tips on Writings College Essay

  • Forget the word limit. Write all your thought process and stories you want to say.
  • Organized scripts according to topics like personal story, family story, academic achievement, social achievement, personal goals, professional goals, and others.
  • Arrange the scripts to maintain a flow that makes best sense.
  • Cut your words, lines, and paragraphs. Remove or reword story that does not portrays your personality.
  • Have someone review your essay.
  • Keep drafting and rewriting until you have several versions of your essay.

Reading books, novels, and previous essays can help you generate ideas on writing and communicating effectively through your essay. Revisit your essay draft if it answers your questions like who you are, what you love, how you perceive your life and community, what your achievements are and what you envision.

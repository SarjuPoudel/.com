Life in US

As often told, prepare for culture shock! Life in US may be very different than what is portrayed in movies. While you may still see fancy hotels and tall buildings, you might find the community or the way people talk or behave different than what you expect. Their accents, interactions, fashions, festivals, and perspectives may differ widely than what you thought before coming to US.

Be a good listener, focus on your studies, and immerse yourself into the new lifestyle of the United States. At last, enjoy every moment and engage in all the fun US has to offer.

College Accpetance

Congratulation! You made it here. Pat yourself on your back and relax and enjoy this moment. Go on a trip with your friends or go help somebody else in their application.

After receiving acceptance from the college, there are few more things that needs to be taken care to attend the college. First of all, read every detail of your acceptance letter. Acknowledge the awarded financial aid if any. Some colleges may offer you a conditional acceptance, so work on negotiating or fulfilling those conditions on your admission. Following actions may be generally required after receiving your acceptance letter.

  • Filling out the financial aid application, if not already.
  • Sending the signed acceptance letter back to the college.
  • Writing a thank you note or essay to your sponsors (usually if sponsor are associated to your college.
  • Application to the college housing.
  • Paying for your admission confirmation fee/housing deposit.
  • Paying your health insurance.
  • Scheduling a meeting with the academic advisor and registaring for classes.
  • Submitting your passport, visa information (if any), Certification of Finances, Bank Statement(s) and immunization documents.
  • Contacting the Designated School Official (DSO) of your college for the I20.
  • Taking a placement exam.

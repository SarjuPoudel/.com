Some Questions To Assists Your College Search

    Based on Immediate Needs
  1. What majors are you interested in? and Why?
  2. How much financial contribution can you make for your college?
  3. Do they offer Scholarships?
  4. Do they offer an application fee waiver? or accept the Common Application?
  5. What is their SAT/TOEFL requirements?
    Based on Auxiliary Needs
  1. What are your career goals?Academic, Research or Industrial/Corporate Job?
  2. What is your monetary goals for the future?
  3. What kind of residential area do you like? City, Remote or Suburban?
  4. Do the college have career services? Are there internship opportunities around the college?
  5. Are you accustom to extreme climate like snow or burning heat?
  6. Is there a community with people from your country?
  7. What is the living expenses like in your college location?
  8. How accessible is the surrounding locations for daily travel?
  9. Is the neighbourhood safe?

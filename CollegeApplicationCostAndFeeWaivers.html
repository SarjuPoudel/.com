College Application: Cost and Fee Waivers

Applying to US colleges can be costly. US College Application fee can range from $0 to $100 dollars per college. Provided the cost of one college application is $80 on average, it may limit your ability to apply to "too" many US colleges. Therefore, it is very wise to make a conservative list of your college choice based on application fee and potential scholarship. It may seem daunting to look at the college application cost. However, there is a Good News. "Colleges provide Fee Waivers to eligible students." Member Colleges using third party services in general offer fee waivers. Some Individual colleges offer fee waivers to financially disadvantage students, if requested and/or attested by your Guidance Counselor (GC) about your financial situation.

    Ways to Reduce Cost of College Application

  • Check if your college is member of Third Party Application services like CommonApp, Universal College Application, and Coalition For College
  • Request Fee waiver by writing an email to admission department of your college.
  • Ask you Guidance Counselor to request a fee waiver on your behalf attesting your financial situation.
  • Attain Online Webinars hosted by colleges, where they like offer Fee Waiver Code.
  • Reconsider your college list, if your total college application fees are beyond your estimated budget.
  • If your are Opportunity Fund Participants of USEF Nepal, check with your advisors if there are any alternatives.

Colleges belonging to Apply Texas may not provide fee waiver for international students. However, a single application can be used to apply to all member colleges of Apply Texas. Additionally, individual colleges may not provide you with a waiver, so you may need to weigh against the probability of your admission and scholarship available in such colleges.

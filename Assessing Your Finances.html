Assessing Your Finances

Financial assesstment refers to the calculation of your ability to pay for your college education. Your present financial circumtances may affect the ability to apply or attend a college in US. US colleges are expensive and your cost of attendance (COA) of college may vary from $0 to $75000 per year. Financial assesstment is suggested to be made based on your or sponsor's currently available monetary resources. Admission to some colleges depends on how much money you or your sponsor put into your education.

Thought Process for a Practical Financial Assessment

  1. What is your or your sponsor's current saving?
  2. What is your sponsor's annual income?
  3. How much worth of your or your sponsor's assests is available for you education?
  4. How many members are their in your family?
  5. What is the expenses for each member now and during your college time?
  6. What percentage of assest or income are you willing to invest in your education?

While assessing your saving and income, you should consider your predeparture expenses like application fee, testing fees, airfare, and shopping expenses.

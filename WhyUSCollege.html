Why US College?

Education system in US makes US College unique than colleges in South Asian countries like Nepal.

Nepal Education System

Nepal's Education system has focused on gaining theoretical knowledge. Such education system equips students with knowledge of science behind a process but fails to provide develop skills that makes students ready for the job or career immediately following the graduation.

US Education System

US Education provides skills that can be use in the modern workforce market. The practical based education system encourage you to think critically and problem solve the complex problem faced by society and the world. The flexibility in switching across the majors or having a multidisciplinary degree makes US education distinct than education in south asian countries like Nepal. In addition, the US education enriches interpersonal, social, and political knowledge of an individual in world's perspective.

While the foundational differences in these education systems exist,both of the education systems have been good in developing human resources for local and global job markets. Therefore, it is important to look into what form of education you would like to receive.

College Application

College Application are forms containing your personal, academic and financial information that are submitted to the college. Colleges may have their own unique form or may use a third party services in order to receive your application. Some of the popular application form listed below.

    Third Party Services

  • CommonApp
  • Universal College Application
  • Coalition For College
  • Apply Texas

    Individual College Application

  • MIT Admission
  • Claflin College Application

To know which application to use, please check the individual college websites on how to apply information. Some colleges offer separate application for international students, so it is necessary to confirm that you are filling out the right application for the college.

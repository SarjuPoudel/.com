Why US?

While you all have your unique education and career aspiration which you undeniably will achieve in your future, why should it matter where you study or work? To understand why studying in the US is a better option for you, you may make a comparative analysis of opportunity cost of your available options.

US has been a destination for thousands of students each year for their higher education. US has unique education opportunity compared to other countries. The education in US provides you with knowledge and skills that is resourceful in global workforce. US education exposes you to the latest advances and challenges you to standout and be best of yourself. US is involved globally which makes you aware about world problems and collectively gives you sense of global citizenship.

Besides a world class education, the US also have opportunity of a lasting career for those who wishes to settle here. International students gets opportunities to work after graduation and develop skillset that can be used anywhere in the world. The unique education system and ever growing job market makes US the popular destination for international students like you.

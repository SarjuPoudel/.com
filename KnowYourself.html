Know Yourself

Before understanding where you should go for higher education, it is very imperative to understanding yourself and your priorities in your life. You are a unique individual who have a unique set of experiences and circumtances that brought you to this phase of the life. So how do you know yourself? To know yourself, you may try by questioning and answering some self reflective questions.

    Personalilty Related
  • Who are you in your family?
  • What responsibilities do you have? in your family? in your community? or in your country?
  • How do you measure your success?
  • What makes you happy?

    • Enonomy Related
  • What is your financial situation?
  • How easy/difficult it has been for you to get your materialistic needs?
  • How did you afford your education so far?
  • How do you plan for paying your further education?

    • Education Related
  • What empowers you to learn more?
  • What is your ideal learning environment?
  • Do you like researching through resources to understand something?
  • What subject do you consider most strong at? and Why?
  • Beside your academic, what are you keen to understanding?

    • Career Related
  • Have you involved in income generating activities before?
  • What do you aspire to be?
  • How do you want to contribute to the society in the future?
  • What are you passionate about doing?

Getting to know yourself better will help you to be grateful for what you already have and strengthen you to face the challenges that lies ahead. No matter what your situation is you will achieve what you want when your goals lie clearly infront of you.
